ROSES




The Rose is a type of flowering shrub.Its name comes from the Latin word "Rosa".The flowers of the rose grow in many different colors,from the well-known red rose or yellow rose and sometimes white or purple roses.Roses belong to the family of plants called Rosaceae.All roses were originally wild and they come from several parts of the world.The wild roses can be grown in gardens,but most garden roses arecultivars,which have been chosen by people.
